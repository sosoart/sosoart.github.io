I have a strong belief that no matter who you are, delicious food makes you happy and it is the best start to experience anything next. 

The idea is that I personally inspired by D&Department store in Japan and 10 Corso Como in Italy which connect and provide integrated experience between food and design in a shop. At the entrance, visitors are stimulated by nice smell of food or scent of coffee. They are already enough to make people feel better. What if they even have some food? People definitely feel good and are openminded *Influence: Robert B. Cialdini. 

With the context, people accept the following experience better and are more engaged. and it was the trigger idea to run the restaurant myself. For the reason, I worked in restaurant more than 6months in person to experience how kitchen works. 

After that, I found a parter to run the shop together and we could open two restaurants and finally connected between food: IM and culture: MOIM.

The name of the shop is ‘Im’ and I believe, it is limitless, because after the name, anything can be placed. For instance, Im’ burger, I’m designer and I’m coffee are possible so that I thought, a number of various names and identities are feasible. Because of that reason, the color theme is not really fixed with certain ones but colorful. Plus, the logo has three lines below to give some blank imagination to fill anything you like as I just described above.

Plus, through the burger box, customers can check menu without menu board and even order through it. Thanks to that, people working for the restaurant can easily remember what customer ordered. 

After that customers are finally served yummy food with the box written by a chef or clerk in person thus they can communicate each other delightfully. Furthermore, it shows what type of food it is and main ingredients of the menu. Throughout the interactive burger box customers are engaged more with the experience in the restaurant and people working as well. 

The idea came out from several interviews by stakeholders; chefs or clerks who sometimes forget what customer ordered and customers who want to know more about food they ordered.

Of course, the most important thing for restaurant is the taste of food. Apart from the point, the most significant issue that I considered was branding of the restaurant as a designer. For that, I collaborated with several artists, Chae who are specializing in calligraphy in Japan and burger art team, Fast&Furious burger in France.

In order to engage customers with the restaurant menu, I normally use quotes that other people normally think as well. Plus, humorous expression with trendy copywrite and colorful objects convey familiar atmosphere which engaging readers more.

The picture above is about video shooting for regional broadcast in Ulsan to show how to cook burgers even at home. The intension was providing various and funny experience of burgers because the menu itself is not familiar with my hometown yet. 

Finally, many people became fans of the burger shop and have been naturally visiting the gallery under the restaurant. It is not a huge success yet but we are very pleased that more and more people visit both places and experience the connection between food and art.
