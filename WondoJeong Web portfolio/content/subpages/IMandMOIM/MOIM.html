It doesn't mean that the purpose is to be better off. The focused purpose is what we wish to do and it should bring the economic support of course to be sustainable. 

If people like to gather and create each one’s value, they can establish the street without monthly rent, companies without non-regular position, working combining work and play and finally even the whole city. Can’t we? Yes. we can! 

The life these days is tough for us even dreaming. However, I would like to be with you as long as you have a hope that you eager to do.

Needs matrix on the opening day. 

We asked visitors: people who want to come together in order to do what they want to do and just looking around, to write what they would like to do except what they are doing now for living but for happiness. Also, they are asked to write or even draw whatever for stimulating active performance. 

Eventually and thankfully, a number of opinions(Sometimes just funny sketches but still precious!) are collected and we are planning on making them in real throughout lectures or workshops in order to let people do what they wish to do.

The first project of MOIM is ‘The gallery on the grassland’ and it is consist of several artists. The reason why we begun from the art is that there are so many artists who are struggling with financial problems so that they rather work in factories or doing totally different works that never wanted to do. Therefore we built on an underground but hopeful space for them to work and even exhibit their works to promote themselves freely. 

As a result of providing free space for artistic people, they can have their own workplace finally to do what they love and also expect economic profits through exhibitions and selling art pieces or designed goods with their art works. In addition, we connect artists to several events made by government which provides not only economic supports but a chance to spread their names out.

For now we are only consist of artists but the goal is that we make the value for the society through what we love and can do. 

The place, I hope, is filled with people who paint, want to bake a pottery, eager to design the city better and make an awesome project proposals. For the reason I am already being thrilled even with imagine that various people come together and do what they really love and finally it brings not only self satisfaction but the precious value even for the society at the end.
