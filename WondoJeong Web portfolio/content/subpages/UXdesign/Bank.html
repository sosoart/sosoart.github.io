Bank of the future.

Creating the new banking experience with four innovative and handy services in the era of physical banks disappearing.

←
→

I do believe in that more people do not visit banks branches to deal with their financing activities especially they are simple and easy tasks. On the other hand a number of users using their smart phone or tablet PC to handle banking services. In addition, more people require faster and simpler ways to use the financing services. With this phenomenon, we created several advanced banking service contents, Investors board, Micro trading, Quick pay, Social account, Money jars and so on.

In order to design the service, the team researched what people do normally about banking and found major activities and observed behaviors. With them, I suggested the new 12 banking solutions.

The infographic above is about the main touch points and users’ behaviors according to them and the last ones are about expected usages in 2016.

As the goal was designing financing service, the core value in terms of UI design was showcasing elements accurately but easily to recognize simultaneously. As a result of the interaction design including visual tasks, the users easily track the flow of usage seamlessly yet still the information was professionally kept.
