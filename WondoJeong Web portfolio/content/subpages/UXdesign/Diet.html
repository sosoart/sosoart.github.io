The real diet.

Before you begin a diet, know yourself, find people like you and eat wisely. Even after all, you may not lose your weight but you are in shape.

←
→

The service aims three goal for people who want a diet in terms of nutrition approach.
Firstly, they support people to achieve not only a goal weight but to maintain the same weight with proper behavior.
Secondly, the appropriate diet program let people to not to worry about numbers; Every single calories of food that they consumed and allow to slim specific part of a body. For example, if you have a big belly, the program target the stomach part especially.
Lastly, it doesn’t force to eat just healthy food that doesn’t consider everyone’s taste It consider what I prefer to eat than green fresh salad only.

Throughout business model canvas, our team strengthen the product to use, sell, spread and sustainable.

The bar chart is the analysis of two different experience about diet in analog and digital.

The goal is achieving only advantages from both such as high-motivation for diet check in person or frequent review with app or website.

The patient journey above shows four main behaviors of patients and 12 key functions according to them.

Last but not least, the app provides seven main functions, Profile of a user to track personal data, Meal planner that suits me to be healthy and I like to consume, Social function, Shopping list to make the list healthy and wisely, Peer to peer that I can find similar users to check how they deal with diet, Panic list which explains obstacle situation to keep me on a diet and how to escape from it and finally Blog feeding useful information about a diet.
