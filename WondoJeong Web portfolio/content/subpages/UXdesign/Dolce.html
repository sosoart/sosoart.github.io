Shopping in the very near future.

Enhancing shopping experience seamlessly for luxurious brand: Dolce and Gabbana through proximity marketing approach.

←
→

The service begun with two main concepts between ‘let people wear first.’ and ‘Still luxurious positioning but friendly.’ that we found needs through firsthand interviewing shoppers.  

The shopping experience and scenarios are considered all touch points from home to inshore and even after the shopping.

In order to understand every stakeholders better, I made shoppers journey map with just basic roles then defined behaviors afterwards.
Both roles and behaviors start between international shoppers who plan on shopping already and emotional shoppers who shop impulsively.

Our team predicted one of the key trend of retail industry: Proximity marketing.  

Based on IoT technology, the proximity marketing provide experience with three steps to persuade more shoppers; Attract people’s attention, convert their behaviors and analyze shoppers patterns.
