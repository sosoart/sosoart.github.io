Even an oven cooks
easy and quick.

The collaboration project between Esselunga and Emile Henry to encourage people to cook food with potteries in the oven.
Easy and Quick.

←
→

Since the project targets slow food culture, its visual design concept was organic.  

For the reason, I used some elements such as cloth texture, brown colors and a handwriting style fonts to express organic design language.

In order to consider responsive website which is suitable for three devices, I used 12 columns and provided specific quotes to measure and guide the sizes, colors and locations among elements for developers to communicate better.
It was considered for maintaining the website to not to have further-small requests to fill contents without developers. It is because, we were supposed to add a number of recipes more following.

The project was the first one that I collaborated with developers firsthand and brought succesful output as a design, thanks to fluent communication between both I: designer and the developer. Finally it became my first real product on the web http://www.esselunga-eh.it/
