Genius: Retail experience design.

Connecting shopping experience between online and offline in genius ways.

←
→

The project has been continuing in the longest period and produced the most-genius services in terms of retail service ranging from digital experience to loyalty strategy. 

As shoppers, they can use every devices among computer, tablet pc and handset to shop in every supermarkets online. Also people can enjoy a number of benefits in-store with a membership.

Since the service was strongly focused on marketing strategy, the marketing faculty and service designers were sticked together to build up well-made retail service. 

The image above explains the process of loyalty strategy from marketing automation; Red-orange to specific approaches: Orange color.

In order to organize thoughts and each pages, a detailed wireframe was created as above. 

Technically, it has 8 layers. First of all, the app shows splash page to give brand identity with Genius logo in the middle then users are asked to fill a form on Login page.
Once the login process is done, people are informed how to use the app with walk-through then, finally, they can use the app. The app has five main menus. List is showing the list of all products monthly in supermarkets, Map to find shops nearby, Add is to add shopping list, Poll is a tool to achieve users opinions and Profile.
