Cups?

Share cups of coffee with someone who you love.
Find the most relevant cafe.
Save your time and money easy and funny.

←
→

The app was specifically designed for Italian coffee culture that people drink coffee several times together during the day. However, it also considered other coffee lovers especially Koreans that the fastest expanding coffee customer in the world. 

The main function of the app starts with profiling to provide relevant and contexture information for users. In addition, there is picking up service which is created for people who consider the time a lot. I was inspired by Koreans who have a tendency to use time effectively. 

Throughout the loyalty system working mainly as gamafication, coffee lovers become engaged the app with economic rewards.

By interviewing some people who love to have coffee, the site map above was made. After that our team could divide some of functions based on kinds of people such as workers, students and so on. After that they were categorized by core behaviors.

The name of the app is ‘Cups?’ It is because it starts from sharing coffee moment with other coffee lovers with simple suggestion. For example, I can simply ask others with single button on my smartphone then we can enjoy coffee in person together. Finally it connects online to offline experience seamlessly.
