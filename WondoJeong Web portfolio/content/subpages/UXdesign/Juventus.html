For 12th players.

Conveying better experience and a culture for Juventus supporters: Juve
through the second screen design.

←
→

The service provides second screen experience that viewers can enjoy the additional contents about watching football game through extra displays such as ipad. 

It is basically designed as dashboard type UI and the app strongly focuses on entertaining roles for soccer fans. One of the main contain is Fancam. I believe in almost every supporters has his/her own favorite player in a team and sometimes wants to see only the player.
For that, the player wears the special uniform which has mounted camera so that the viewer can watch very realistic play as if he is playing the game.

In order to understand what supporters want and do weekly, it is because they have certain behaviors according to the match usually happens on Sunday, I made a supporters journey.
After that, there were a number of opportunities to bring better watching experience so that I positioned them on the opportunity map based on four standards; Controlling, Enjoying, Personal function and social function.

Through several research methods, we finally summed up the sitemap and some rough sketches related with it. Through these, the service was reviewed and discussed with some of true Juventus supporters and all of them loved it very much.
After that, I added auction function thanks to a supporter gave me as an idea through focus group interviewing. Finally, sickle auction was created and it became one of the powerful business model of the 12th Juventus.
