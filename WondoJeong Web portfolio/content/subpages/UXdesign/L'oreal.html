Connecting customers and hairshops.

Know what hair designers need then they can provide better services.Know what customers need after that they are provided proper contents.

←
→

The service targets both users between retailers: hair designers and visitors and provide appropriate contents based on profiling. 

As retailers, they are asked some fundamental information that customers are normally curious about. Thus there is no such a miscommunication. For example, customer always can check opening hours to not to go when the shop closes without visiting thanks to hair-shops obligatorily putting information.

On the other hand, customers feel convenient to find shops through a heat map then they can check details of shops and are even possible to book with smartphone. Every activities are returning as rewards via loyalty program such as gamification.

The project was quite complicated at the beginning, even if the outcome was single mobile app. It is because the target users are both retailers and customers. Plus it requires to contemplate loyalty program, proximity marketing and so on. .

As a result of researches, our team found six main opportunities and could divided retailers into three loyalty membership according to users’ behaviors.

Every contents are relevant so users are easily engaged with the service. For example, users are provided relevant contents on homepage after profiling. Additionally, the map demonstrated shops near by me and related with my interests or status.  .

Finally, the service is contextual. For instance, the app provides relevant information or promotion when the customers are near by. It is because beacons recognize the locations of users and provide proper contents thanks to their activities on their handset or any internet-connected tools.
