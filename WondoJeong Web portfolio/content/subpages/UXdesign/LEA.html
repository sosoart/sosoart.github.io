Books and more.

The first European book streaming platform design and it was proudly released to the international book fair.

←
→

The goal of the project was to readers own Laterza, One of the biggest and historical publication company in Italy, books on their tablet PC and computer through native and web app in order to let readers can read books whenever.

As the name of the app LEA: Libri e altro(Read and more in Italy), it provides the most convenient reading environment as a matter of course, other useful functions and contents. For example seamless synchronizing function what and where I have been reading through two devices, possibility to discuss with other readers and even authors, accessing of behind story of the book and related media about books such as the music as BGM.

In addition, the application had to be considered by multiple aspects. It is because the app should play roles to search books, authors and provide reading task which includes communication/ discussion function with other readers even on the single line in a page of a book and so on.

In order to not to miss such fundamental but crucial functions to reflect elements described above, I strongly focus on customer journey for analyzing readers’ behaviors and built information architecture for proper contents.

After all, there were a number of usability tests to double check to provide convenient reading experience.

At the end, the project ended successfully and was presented in Internation book fair in Turin and published on WIRED. Now it is available online to use web app and apple store for downloading native app.
