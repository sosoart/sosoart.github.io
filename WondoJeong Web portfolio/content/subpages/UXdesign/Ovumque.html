My digital butler.

Ovumque is the home management system based on IoT(Internet of things) that you can easily plug and play.

←
→

Throughout a small egg-shaped hub, users can build their house care system easily and smart. 

In order to install, you just need to plug an extra plug and just enjoy the smart house management system. 

In order to reduce the costs, it works as modular hardware and software while people using . In addition, it handles four network protocols simultaneously. Also it provides open development platform to allow any community(developers) to invent the new and advanced solutions.

As you can check the infographic above, Ovumque supports any devices to control itself.
Through them, the user can control electronic devices remotely, checking what he has in the fridge and when he put them, controlling thermostat to keep the house warm and cool before entering home.
Finally the user can track each bill ranged from electronic, water bills and so on with his handset.
