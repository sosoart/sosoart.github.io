SNS: Students network service.

The new type of social network service specifically for students who have different purposes from general SNS such as Facebook or too professional based social network service like Linkedin. They need this.

←
→

The app is additional service from 10mins with: Social network service among students to share ideas, discuss common interests or find job after graduation. 

Qulefie, in fact, focuses on more about asking and answering among students to bond themsleves better and stronger in educational aspects.

Every contents can be made with taking videos and recording voice which are more familiar and easier methods among young generation than just texting these days.

Technically, it was my first project using sketch3 which is the most powerful design tool that is good for not only designing visual elements for digital products accurately but communicating with developers.

As a result of satisfying works between designers and developers, the outcome brought outstanding interface quality and I believe in the well-made app could provide enhanced experience in terms of usability for students.
