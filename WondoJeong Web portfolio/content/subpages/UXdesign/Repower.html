Energy. Under your control.

Powerful energy controller through any smart devices supported by IoT. You can check energy consumption , analyze patterns, predict it and control it.

←
→

The core value of the project was building up the system to check the energy consumption among facilities in a company, control some functions, if it is necessary and even predict; Feed forward.
The colors were reflected their brand color palette: Red and blue.

Since the app deals with technical elements and financial value, the design language should be accurate and was supposed to be easy to analyze contents. 

Fulfilling both different issues was quite challenging at the beginning.

However, it, finally became sophisticated and easily recognizable design through proper call to action hints and several infographic approaches.

The system works based on proximity sensor. For instance, the user approaches at a certain factory, it shows general information about it. After that, he enters the facility and the app, now, demonstrates the information about the specific machine. 

So that the user is provided contextual information therefore he is easily engaged with the contents better.
