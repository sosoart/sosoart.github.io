National reconstruction project.

Enhancing outdated real-estate assets to reduce energy consumption, pollution and even budget for maintaining house as a long term plan.

The goal of the project was building up the website to promote the Road to 2020 campaign. 

It was designed as responsive website so it demonstrates contents effectively among three devices; Computer, iPad and iPhone. Also it has parallel effect thus each key values naturally continues.

These days, 50% of Italian real-estate assets are outdated and 40% of them cause atmospheric pollution. Additionally, that sort of old building spend 47% more energy since 2003 and it will grow 30% more in 5 years. 

These are the background and the serious problems we faced.

In order to solve the problem, Road to 2020: the sustainable ecosystem launched.

Firstly, the reasonable price of sensors are installed in the building and inhabitants use smart devices to check the system of a place. Additionally, it can deal with several-comfortable life styles such as purchasing food with an app to home.
