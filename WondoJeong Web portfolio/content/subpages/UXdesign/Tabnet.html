Parking is not difficult anymore.

The service provides the easiest way to find parking lots relevant and contextual and payment system.

←
→

Even if it is an era of smart phone, people still use kiosks to pay for parking cars these days in Italy. 

With this phenomenon, the app was designed to provide better experience to park and pay the bill throughout smart phones easily and faster.

While research team was having a meeting, we found the typical Italian life style that some people are not always connected to internet and some places totally block the internet signal. Thus we designed the process which even works without the internet such as SMS notification.

There are two kinds of users using parking lot between using timer that people can set the time and pay with the amount of the time and it alerts with the gage filling down according to time left. On the other hand, if the driver uses stopwatch, it starts the time spending and he/ she just pays as much as parking time.

Since the app has to deal with the payment system, it has quite complicated wireframe. 

In case users want to enroll the regular card and want to pay with it, they put some essential information and using parking service as I mentioned above; Timer and stopwatch. Whereas, some don’t want to register the credit then they can use Oneshot payment method. 

The service is connected to any places especially tabaccheria(Tabac shop in Italian).Customers go to any spaces dealing with the service and set the amount on their smart phone then it becomes barcode and pay. After all the user can recharge as much as he wants then starts to park straight away with it.
